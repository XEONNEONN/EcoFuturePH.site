Learn about global warming and how we can save our planet.

What is Global Warming?

Global warming is the increase in Earth's temperature due to greenhouse gases.

Causes

  • Burning fossil fuels
  • Deforestation
  • Industrial pollution

Effects

  • Rising sea levels
  • Stronger storms
  • Heatwaves

Impact in the Philippines

Climate change causes floods, typhoons, and crop damage in the Philippines.

Solutions

  • Use renewable energy
  • Plant trees
  • Reduce waste

What You Can Do

  • Save electricity
  • Avoid plastic
  • Educate others
